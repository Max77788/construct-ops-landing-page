How It Works

One system. All projects.
All locations.

From budget upload to billing package — every step owned, automated, and audit-trailed.

1
Upload your budget file
PM uploads the standardized Excel budget. System parses vendors, contract values, cost codes, retainage terms, and timeline. Project workspace and live ledger created instantly. This is the foundation everything runs on.
2
System opens billing cycle
On the configured date (typically the 20th), automated reminders go out via SMS and email to every vendor. Reminder on the 23rd. Hard cutoff on the 25th. No human follow-up required.
3
Subs submit in under 2 minutes
Sub clicks their secure magic link — no app download, no password. They see their contract context, enter line-item amounts, upload one backup doc, and sign digitally. Done.
4
PM reviews and approves the batch
One screen shows all vendors: submitted, missing, over-budget. PM approves, edits with audit trail, or rejects with a comment. Every action logged. No spreadsheet, no email thread.
5
Billing package generated, ledger updated
One click generates the Excel and PDF billing package — formatted to your template, backup docs bundled, retainage calculated. Ledger updates automatically. SharePoint filed. Cash cycle closed.
